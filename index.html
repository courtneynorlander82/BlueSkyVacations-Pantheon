Theme Design Notes

During our meeting using WebEx Courtney, Matt and Ken met with Pamela online to listen to her requests for her business's website. In this meeting we understood the following about her business...

We met with our client Pamela on 6/28/2016.

  • This is a small start up business ran by Pamela in Michigan who is a licensed realtor.
  • At this current time there is no web site for Blue Sky Vacations.
  • Pamela would like to maintain her website as time goes on.
  • Pamela want's to increase awareness for the business.
  • There are 2 target audiences for this business, the renters and the property owners.
  • We have a domain name which is blueskyvacationofmichigan.com however we need to figure out a hosting service for her.
  • Pamelas main concern was making the homepage most important with lots of color and animation and life to it.
  • There is lots of competition so we need to make this site stand out for her business.
  • We need to make the rentals appealing to renters in a tough market.

WordPress Plug-ins

Ultimate Responsive Image Slider

  • Installed by Ken Anich
  • UR Image Slider Info
  • Pros:
  • Includes many included features.
  • Customizable sizes and fonts will allow for multiple uses within our site, such as for property listings and thumbnails.
  • Images are easy to download, organize, and place in order.
  • Cons:
  • The Fruitful theme we selected for our site already has an image slider.
  • The free features may limit our ability to do some advanced techniques or satisfy the client's needs.
  • There would be a fee to access any premium features.

WooCommerce

Installed by Matt Allen

WooCommerce

  • Pros:
  • One of the leading ecommerce plugins for WordPress.
  • Very feature rich that can scale to handle many different businesses.
  • Orders and Invoicing / Payments / Shipping / Shopping-Cart / Checkout: All included.
  • Extensive Support through community / support staff.

  • Cons:
  • Needs additional plugins to handle properties / services sales.
  • Not compatible with certain themes / may require additonal coding for setup.

WP-DB-Backup

  • Installed by Courtney Norlander
  • Backup information
  • Pros:
  • Works with other plugins with ease.
  • Updated frequently and easy to install.
  • It works with a database easily and swiftly.
  • Cons:
  • It says right on the website that there is no warranty.
  • Its basic. There are no frills or extras with it.
